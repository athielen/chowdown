ingredients:
- 1/2 cup butter, melted
- 1 teaspoon garlic powder
- 1/2 teaspoon onion salt
- 1/4 teaspoon black pepper, freshly ground
- 1/2 cup parmesan cheese, grated

directions:
- Add all ingredients to a bowl and whisk together.
