ingredients:
- 100ml Prosecco (Chilled)
- 40ml Mandarin Juice
- No Garnish

directions:
- Squeeze fresh mandarin juice through a seize into a champagne glass. 
- Slowly add chilled prosecco or a dry brut sparkling white wine equivalent. No garnish.
